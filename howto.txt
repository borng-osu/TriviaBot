                        TRIVIABOT COMMANDS

BASIC KEYWORDS

What/Who is ___?
            Replace ___ with query to receive wiki summary.

Today
            Get today's date.

Tell me a joke.
            Hear a funny joke!

ADVANCED KEYWORDS

How many x are in y?
            Find out how many x are in y via wiki query.

When was x y-ed?
            Find out the date for when an action, y, occurred to x.
